Explore Ohrid

Ohrid is a city full of wonders waiting to be explored. Nestled on the shores of one of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes, it offers breathtaking landscapes and a rich cultural heritage. With its 365 churches, including the iconic St. John at Kaneo, the majestic monastery of St. Naum, and the historic Samuel's Fortress, Ohrid is a true open-air museum.
