Samuel's Fortress

A historic landmark offering stunning views of Ohrid and the lake, a key part of Macedonia's cultural heritage. This ancient fortress, built in the 10th century, once served as a stronghold for the medieval kingdom of Samuil, adding to its significance as a symbol of Ohrid's rich history

St. John at Kaneo

A stunning 13th-century church perched above the lake, offering breathtaking views of Ohrid and its surroundings. Known for its unique architecture and picturesque setting, it is one of the most iconic landmarks in Macedonia and a symbol of Ohrid's rich spiritual heritage

Historic Part of Ohrid

A UNESCO World Heritage site, the historic center of Ohrid is a treasure trove of ancient monuments, churches, and cobblestone streets. Steeped in history, it offers a glimpse into the past with its well-preserved Byzantine and medieval architecture.

  • Price: 25€

Bay of the Bones

An archaeological site on the shores of Lake Ohrid, featuring a reconstructed ancient settlement built on stilts. This unique open-air museum allows visitors to explore the lifestyle of prehistoric people who lived here over 3,000 years ago, offering fascinating insights into the past.
